Rock Paper Scissors is a simple 2-player game typically played using the hands. Players take turns forming one of three shapes to indicate their choice: rock (using a closed fist), paper (using a flat hand), or scissors (a closed fist with two outstretched fingers, resembling a peace sign).

Each choice beats one other choice, is defeated by another choice, and in the event that the other player picks the same choice, results in a tie.
